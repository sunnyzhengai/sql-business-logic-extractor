total_discharges
  Count of records
  Technical: COUNT(*)

readmissions
  Sum of flagged.is_30day_readmit
  Source: flagged
  Technical: SUM(flagged.is_30day_readmit)

readmit_rate_pct
  Count of records
  Source: flagged
  Technical: CAST(SUM(flagged.is_30day_readmit) AS FLOAT) / COUNT(*) * 100

avg_los
  Average of flagged.los_days
  Source: flagged
  Technical: AVG(flagged.los_days)

early_readmits
  Conditional count of records where flagged.readmit_category equals 'Early'
  Source: flagged
  Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN flagged.readmit_category = 'Early' THEN 1 END)

late_readmits
  Conditional count of records where flagged.readmit_category equals 'Late'
  Source: flagged
  Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN flagged.readmit_category = 'Late' THEN 1 END)

los_days
  The number of days between Hospital admission date and time and Hospital discharge date and time
  Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
  Condition: Hospital discharge date and time is not null
  Condition: Patient class (1=Inpatient, 2=Outpatient, 3=Emergency, 4=Observation) is Inpatient
  Condition: Hospital admission date and time is at least '2025-01-01'
  Technical: DATEDIFF(day, pat_enc_hsp.hosp_admsn_time, pat_enc_hsp.hosp_disch_time)

encounter_seq
  Row ranking (used for deduplication or ordering)
  Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
  Condition: Hospital discharge date and time is not null
  Condition: Patient class (1=Inpatient, 2=Outpatient, 3=Emergency, 4=Observation) is Inpatient
  Condition: Hospital admission date and time is at least '2025-01-01'
  Technical: ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY pat_enc_hsp.pat_id ORDER BY pat_enc_hsp.hosp_admsn_time)

next_admit_time
  Previous/next value comparison using index_encounters.PAT_ID, index_encounters.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME, index_encounters.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME
  Source: index_encounters
  Technical: LEAD(index_encounters.hosp_admsn_time) OVER (PARTITION BY index_encounters.pat_id ORDER BY index_encounters.hosp_admsn_time)

days_to_readmit
  Previous/next value comparison using DAY, index_encounters.HOSP_DISCH_TIME, index_encounters.PAT_ID, index_encounters.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME, index_encounters.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME
  Source: index_encounters
  Technical: DATEDIFF(day, index_encounters.hosp_disch_time, LEAD(index_encounters.hosp_admsn_time) OVER (PARTITION BY index_encounters.pat_id ORDER BY index_encounters.hosp_admsn_time))

is_30day_readmit
  Classified as:
  - 1 when with_readmit.days_to_readmit is at most 30
  - 0 otherwise
  Source: with_readmit
  Technical: CASE WHEN with_readmit.days_to_readmit <= 30 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END

readmit_category
  Classified as:
  - 'Early' when with_readmit.days_to_readmit is at most 7
  - 'Late' when with_readmit.days_to_readmit is between 8 and 30
  - 'None' otherwise
  Source: with_readmit
  Technical: CASE WHEN with_readmit.days_to_readmit <= 7 THEN 'Early' WHEN with_readmit.days_to_readmit BETWEEN 8 AND 30 THEN 'Late' ELSE 'None' END
