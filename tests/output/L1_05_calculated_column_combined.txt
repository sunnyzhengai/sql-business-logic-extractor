======================================================================
  L1_05_calculated_column
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  PAT_MRN_ID,
  DATEDIFF(YEAR, BIRTH_DATE, GETDATE()) AS age
FROM PATIENT

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  age
    The number of years between birth_date and GETDATE()
    Technical: DATEDIFF(year, birth_date, GETDATE())

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------
  PAT_MRN_ID (passthrough)
    Resolved: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base columns: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base tables: PATIENT

  age (calculated)
    Resolved: DATEDIFF(YEAR, BIRTH_DATE, GETDATE())
    Base columns: PATIENT.YEAR, PATIENT.BIRTH_DATE
    Base tables: PATIENT
