======================================================================
  L1_08_left_join
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  e.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID,
  dx.DX_NAME
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP AS e
LEFT JOIN HSP_ACCT_DX_LIST AS dl
  ON e.HSP_ACCOUNT_ID = dl.HSP_ACCOUNT_ID
LEFT JOIN CLARITY_EDG AS dx
  ON dl.DX_ID = dx.DX_ID
WHERE
  dl.LINE = 1

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  filter:where:HSP_ACCT_DX_LIST.LINE
    Filter: only include records where Diagnosis priority line (1=Primary, 2=Secondary, etc.) equals 1
    Source: HSP_ACCT_DX_LIST (Diagnosis list for hospital accounts (primary, secondary, etc.))
    Technical: hsp_acct_dx_list.line = 1

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------
  PAT_ENC_CSN_ID (passthrough)
    Resolved: PAT_ENC_HSP.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
    Base columns: PAT_ENC_HSP.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
    Base tables: PAT_ENC_HSP
    Filter: dl.LINE = 1

  DX_NAME (passthrough)
    Resolved: CLARITY_EDG.DX_NAME
    Base columns: CLARITY_EDG.DX_NAME
    Base tables: CLARITY_EDG
    Filter: dl.LINE = 1
