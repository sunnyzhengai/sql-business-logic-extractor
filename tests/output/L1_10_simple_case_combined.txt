  L1_10_simple_case
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  PAT_MRN_ID,
  CASE
    WHEN AGE_YEARS < 18
    THEN 'Pediatric'
    WHEN AGE_YEARS BETWEEN 18 AND 64
    THEN 'Adult'
    ELSE 'Geriatric'
  END AS age_group
FROM PATIENT

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  age_group
    Classified as:
  - 'Pediatric' when age_years is less than 18
  - 'Adult' when age_years is between 18 and 64
  - 'Geriatric' otherwise
    Technical: CASE WHEN age_years < 18 THEN 'Pediatric' WHEN age_years BETWEEN 18 AND 64 THEN 'Adult' ELSE 'Geriatric' END

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------
  PAT_MRN_ID (passthrough)
    Resolved: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base columns: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base tables: PATIENT

  age_group (case)
    Resolved: CASE WHEN AGE_YEARS < 18 THEN 'Pediatric' WHEN AGE_YEARS BETWEEN 18 AND 64 THEN 'Adult' ELSE 'Geriatric' END
    Base columns: PATIENT.AGE_YEARS
    Base tables: PATIENT
