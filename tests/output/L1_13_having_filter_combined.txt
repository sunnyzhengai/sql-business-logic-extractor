======================================================================
  L1_13_having_filter
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  p.PRIMARY_DX_CODE,
  COUNT(*) AS patient_count,
  AVG(e.TOTAL_CHARGES) AS avg_charges
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP AS e
JOIN PATIENT AS p
  ON e.PAT_ID = p.PAT_ID
GROUP BY
  p.PRIMARY_DX_CODE
HAVING
  COUNT(*) >= 10 AND AVG(e.TOTAL_CHARGES) > 5000

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  patient_count
    Count of records
    Technical: COUNT(*)

  avg_charges
    Average of Total charges for the encounter
    Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Condition: AVG(pat_enc_hsp.total_charges) is greater than 5000
    Technical: AVG(pat_enc_hsp.total_charges)

  filter:having:unknown
    Filter: only include records where COUNT(*) is at least 10
    Technical: COUNT(*) >= 10

  filter:having:PAT_ENC_HSP.TOTAL_CHARGES
    Filter: only include records where AVG(pat_enc_hsp.total_charges) is greater than 5000
    Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Technical: AVG(pat_enc_hsp.total_charges) > 5000

----------------------------------------
