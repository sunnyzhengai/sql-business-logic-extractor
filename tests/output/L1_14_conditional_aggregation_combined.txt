======================================================================
  L1_14_conditional_aggregation
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  dep.DEPARTMENT_NAME,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN e.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 1 THEN 1 END) AS discharged_home,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN e.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 2 THEN 1 END) AS transferred,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN e.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 20 THEN 1 END) AS expired,
  COUNT(*) AS total
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP AS e
JOIN CLARITY_DEP AS dep
  ON e.DEPARTMENT_ID = dep.DEPARTMENT_ID
GROUP BY
  dep.DEPARTMENT_NAME

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  discharged_home
    Conditional count of records where Discharge disposition (1=Home, 2=Transfer, 20=Expired) is Home
    Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN pat_enc_hsp.disch_disposition_c = 1 THEN 1 END)

  transferred
    Conditional count of records where Discharge disposition (1=Home, 2=Transfer, 20=Expired) is Transfer to another facility
    Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN pat_enc_hsp.disch_disposition_c = 2 THEN 1 END)

  expired
    Conditional count of records where Discharge disposition (1=Home, 2=Transfer, 20=Expired) is Expired (deceased)
    Source: PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN pat_enc_hsp.disch_disposition_c = 20 THEN 1 END)

  total
    Count of records
    Technical: COUNT(*)

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------

  discharged_home (aggregate)

  transferred (aggregate)
    Resolved: COUNT(CASE WHEN e.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 2 THEN 1 END)

    Resolved: COUNT(CASE WHEN e.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 20 THEN 1 END)
    Base columns: PAT_ENC_HSP.DISCH_DISPOSITION_C
    Base tables: PAT_ENC_HSP

    Resolved: COUNT(*)
