======================================================================
  L1_17_running_total
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  PAT_ENC_CSN_ID,
  SERVICE_DATE,
  TX_AMOUNT,
  SUM(TX_AMOUNT) OVER (
    PARTITION BY PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
    ORDER BY SERVICE_DATE
    ROWS BETWEEN UNBOUNDED PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW
  ) AS cumulative_charges
FROM HSP_TRANSACTIONS

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  cumulative_charges
    Running total of PAT_ENC_CSN_ID, TX_AMOUNT, SERVICE_DATE
    Technical: SUM(tx_amount) OVER (PARTITION BY pat_enc_csn_id ORDER BY service_date ROWS BETWEEN UNBOUNDED PRECEDING AND CURRENT ROW)
