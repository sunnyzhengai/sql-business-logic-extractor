FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  e.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID,
  e.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP AS e
WHERE
  EXISTS(
    SELECT
      1
    FROM ORDER_MED AS om
    WHERE
      om.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID = e.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID AND om.ORDER_STATUS_C = 2
  )

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  filter:where:ORDER_MED.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
    Filter: only include records where EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM order_med AS om WHERE order_med.pat_enc_csn_id = pat_enc_hsp.pat_enc_csn_id AND order_med.order_status_c = 2)
    Source: ORDER_MED (Medication orders), PAT_ENC_HSP (Hospital patient encounters (inpatient, ED, observation))
    Technical: EXISTS(SELECT 1 FROM order_med AS om WHERE order_med.pat_enc_csn_id = pat_enc_hsp.pat_enc_csn_id AND order_med.order_status_c = 2)

  1
    Fixed value
    Technical: 1

  filter:where:ORDER_MED.PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
    Filter: only include records where Encounter identifier (FK to PAT_ENC_HSP) equals e.pat_enc_csn_id
    Source: ORDER_MED (Medication orders), e
    Technical: order_med.pat_enc_csn_id = e.pat_enc_csn_id

  filter:where:ORDER_MED.ORDER_STATUS_C
    Filter: only include records where Order status (1=Pending, 2=Active/Complete, 3=Discontinued) is Active/Complete
    Source: ORDER_MED (Medication orders)
    Technical: order_med.order_status_c = 2
