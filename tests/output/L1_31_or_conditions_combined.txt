======================================================================
  L1_31_or_conditions
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  PAT_MRN_ID
FROM PATIENT
WHERE
  PAT_STATUS = 'Active' OR PAT_STATUS = 'Inactive'

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  filter:where:PAT_STATUS
    Filter: only include records where pat_status equals 'Active' or pat_status equals 'Inactive'
    Technical: pat_status = 'Active' OR pat_status = 'Inactive'

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------
  PAT_MRN_ID (passthrough)
    Resolved: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base columns: PATIENT.PAT_MRN_ID
    Base tables: PATIENT
    Filter: PAT_STATUS = 'Active' OR PAT_STATUS = 'Inactive'
