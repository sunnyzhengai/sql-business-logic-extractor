  L1_33_between_and_in
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  PAT_ENC_CSN_ID
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP
WHERE
  HOSP_ADMSN_TIME BETWEEN '2025-01-01' AND '2025-12-31'
  AND ADT_PAT_CLASS_C IN (1, 2, 3)
  AND NOT DEPARTMENT_ID IN (100, 200)

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  filter:where:HOSP_ADMSN_TIME
    Filter: only include records where hosp_admsn_time is between '2025-01-01' and '2025-12-31'
    Technical: hosp_admsn_time BETWEEN '2025-01-01' AND '2025-12-31'

  filter:where:ADT_PAT_CLASS_C
    Filter: only include records where adt_pat_class_c IN (1, 2, 3)
    Technical: adt_pat_class_c IN (1, 2, 3)

  filter:where:DEPARTMENT_ID
    Filter: only include records where not (department_id IN (100, 200))
    Technical: NOT department_id IN (100, 200)
