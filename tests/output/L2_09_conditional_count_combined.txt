======================================================================
  L2_09_conditional_count
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  COUNT(CASE WHEN DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 1 THEN 1 END) AS discharged_home
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  discharged_home
    Conditional count of records where disch_disposition_c equals 1
    Technical: COUNT(CASE WHEN disch_disposition_c = 1 THEN 1 END)

----------------------------------------

    Resolved: COUNT(CASE WHEN DISCH_DISPOSITION_C = 1 THEN 1 END)
