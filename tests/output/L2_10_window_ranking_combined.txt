======================================================================
  L2_10_window_ranking
======================================================================

FORMATTED SQL:
----------------------------------------
SELECT
  ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY PAT_ID ORDER BY HOSP_ADMSN_TIME DESC) AS rn
FROM PAT_ENC_HSP

BUSINESS DEFINITIONS (Plain English):
----------------------------------------
  rn
    Row ranking (used for deduplication or ordering)
    Technical: ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY pat_id ORDER BY hosp_admsn_time DESC)

LINEAGE (Resolved to base tables):
----------------------------------------
  rn (window)
    Resolved: ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY PAT_ID ORDER BY HOSP_ADMSN_TIME DESC)
    Base columns: PAT_ENC_HSP.PAT_ID, PAT_ENC_HSP.HOSP_ADMSN_TIME
    Base tables: PAT_ENC_HSP
